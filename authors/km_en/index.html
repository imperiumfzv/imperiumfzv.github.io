Kasandra Musović

PhD Student

UM Faculty of Health Sciences

Kasandra Musović is a Teaching Assistant in Nursing at the Faculty of Health Sciences, University of Maribor, and a PhD student in Nursing. Her research field is a mixture of science of caring and nursing education, evidence based nursing and research methods, both quantitative and qualitative. As part of his PhD, she is conducting research on the adaptation of Watson’s Theory of Caring in nursing education.
